SWEPCO 801 Clear Penetrating Lubricant is a premium quality penetrating oil and lubricant. Use it for loosening frozen and corroded bolts, nuts, screws, clamps and other threaded parts. Also good for pins and hinges and other parts that need disassembly. Just spray it on and let it go to work dissolving and loosening rust, corrosion and crud of all kinds. Super fast penetration means more work gets done with less wait time. And the clear formula means less clean-up. After disassembly and reassembly, spray parts to help prevent future rust and corrosion.
