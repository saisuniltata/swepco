SWEPCO 717 Synchromesh Transmission Fluid is a high performance, extended service transmission fluid designed especially for manual synchromesh transmissions and transaxles made by GM, Chrystler, Ford and most European and Japanese manufacturers. The specially balanced formulation provides the correct frictional characteristics and wide temperature range needed for smooth, positive shift quality and long term protection.
