Heavy pounding and slamming associated with shovels, front end loaders, pile drivers, bulldozers and other heavy equipment of this variety quickly break down and pound out conventional greases, leaving bearings and other friction surfaces dry. SWEPCO 110 Shock Resistant Grease has been especially formulated to provide an extra margin of safety in severe service applications where shock loading makes conventional multipurpose and extreme pressure greases inadequate. It is a premium quality, cost cutting grease which combines superior shock resistance with excellent extreme pressure performance and chemical and thermal stability. FEATURES

  • Superior water resistance
  • Rust and corrosion protection
  • Excellent cold weather pumpability
  • Contains SWEPCO's proprietary anti-friction and anti-wear additive, LUBIUM
