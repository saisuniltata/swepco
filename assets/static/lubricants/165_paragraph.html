SWEPCO 165 MolyXP Universal Valve Lubricant is a high performance multi-service lubricant formulated to deliver unsurpassed protection and sealing of a wide range of process, distribution and control valves. SWEPCO's Syntheon synthetic base stocks, over-based calcium sulfonate base and advanced additive chemistry, such as Moly and Lubium II, provide superior lubrication, wear resistance, corrosionnd rust resistance and sealing for all types of industry valves...including those used in caustic chemical, abrasive, high pressure and high temperature service. Insure proper functioning, longer life and less valve-related downtime of your valves with SWEPCO 165.

FEATURES

  • Helps protect metal seated valve surfaces from wear caused by metal to metal contact and abrasive materials
  • Recommended for manual and servo operated gate, ball, and other types of process, control and distribution valves
  • Prevents leaks, entry of abrasives and loss of operating pressures
  • Excellent protection from corrosion caused by water, alcohols, glycols, CO2, H2S, and other solvents and caustic chemicals
  • Compatible with all commonly used seal materials
