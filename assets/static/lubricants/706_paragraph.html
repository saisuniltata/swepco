SWEPCO 706 AW/EP Gear & Bearing Oil is a premium quality, extended service industrial oil formulated to provide superior performance in a wide range of plant, mobile and marine applications. It provides outstanding service in all types of oil delivery configurations, including sump, mist, drip and circulating systems. It is particularly well suited for automatic lubricators and centralized lubrication systems which help plant maintenance personnel obtain the longest life and least lubricant consumption.
