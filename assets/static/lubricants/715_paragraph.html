SWEPCO 715 Power steering/Hydraulic Oil is a new generation high performance oil designed to assure efficient, smooth operation of power-assisted steering and hydraulic systems. It is engineered and blended precisely from the very finest high VI paraffinic base stocks available and the most advanced additive chemistry, including Lubium, a unique chemical additive developed by SWEPCO's R & D Lab for the exclusive use in certain SWEPCO lubricants. SWEPCO 715 Power Steering/Hydraulic Oil is especially formulated to meet requirements for power steering/hydraulic units in U.S. and Imported Autos (excluding Honda... Honda recommends only Honda fluids) FEATURES

  • High VI paraffinic base stock
  • Contains LUBIUM (additive)
  • Anti-foam protection
  • Anti-squeal additive
