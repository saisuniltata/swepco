SWEPCO 702 Synthetic Universal AW Compressor Oil is a high performance, extended service compressor oil formulated to provide superior performance under the most demanding operating conditions in rotary screw, rotary vane, reciprocating and other types of compressors. Compressor manufacturers agree: sludge, varnish and carbon deposits resulting from oxidation of the oil is the number one cause of compressor inefficiencies and failure. SWEPCO 702 is formulated to combat friction and wear that result from high temperatures, water, contaminants and heavy loads...providing much longer equipment and oil life. Lower operating temperatures also extend seal life and reduce oil leakage and consumption. FEATURES

  • High Viscosity Index paraffinic base stock, contains DIMONYL
  • High thermal stability
  • Excellent chemical stability
  • Resistant to emulsification
  • Resistant to rust
  • Resistant to foaming
  • USDA approved for use in closed systems
