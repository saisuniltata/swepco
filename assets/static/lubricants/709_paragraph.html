SWEPCO 709 Universal Tractor Hydraulic oil formulated to satisfy the varying requirements of most major tractor manufacturers. It is designed for use in Agco, John Deere, Kubota, International Harvester, Masey-Ferguson, J.I. Case, Ford, Allis Chalmers, White, Minneapolis-Moline, New Holland and Oliver equipement.

SWEPCO 709 Universal Tractor Hydraulic oil is also a multipurpose hydraulic oil intended for use in hydraulic systems, transmissions, power take-off units, final drive units and wet brake systems. Because of its ability to meet these many varying needs of a great number of major equipement manufacturers, SWEPCO 709 Universal Tractor Hydraulic Oil reduces lubricant inventories requirements, simplifies maintenance procedures, reduces the cost and time of maintenance and helps eliminate lubrication mistakes.
