SWEPCO 402 Roller Lube is designed primarily as a track roller lubricant for crawler–type equipment, but performs well in a great many other applications. It clings to metal, forming a protective film which assures proper lubrication at all times. SWEPCO 402 Roller Lube seals out dirt and water, and prevents costly metal to metal contact, greatly increasing bearing life. In addition, SWEPCO 402 Roller Lube flows readily through tiny entrances to track roller shafts, giving positive lubrication only when needed…when the tractor is in motion. SWEPCO 402 Roller Lube lubricates all the time, at slow and high speeds. FEATURES

  • Excellent All-Temperature Performance
